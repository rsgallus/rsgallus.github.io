A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Ryan, and this is my website. I built this as a project to keep my web skills sharp at a time when I was mostly doing Product Management. Now I post pictures and articles from time to time. I studied Computer Engineering at Iowa State Universtity and then did a Masters of Business Administration. Since then I've worked in Software Engineering and Product Management roles in the ecommerce industry.

Below you'll find pictures from travels, articles about experiences, and some thoughts from my software experience. Learn more about me here.
