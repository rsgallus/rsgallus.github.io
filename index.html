- Hiker and explorer with a passion for the outdoors -

The small island of Iceland is home to some of the world's most incredible geography. Here I try to capture some of the country's powerful waterfalls, unique geothermal areas, and massive glaciers.

European cities are renowned for their incredible architecture. Over several trips, I explore parts of London, Edinburgh, Paris, and Northern Italy.

The National Parks of the United States and Canada draw hundreds thousands of thousands of visitors each year. However, some of the most impressive scenery is only accessible via long hikes.
