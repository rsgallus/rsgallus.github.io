Photos in Europe

European cities are renowned for their incredible architecture. In 2014, I had the opportunity to see most of the major cities in Italy. In 2015, I spent a semester at the University of Birmingham in England, visiting several other countries along the way. I returned to Italy in 2017 but stayed north, around Lake Como. These pictures span parts of London, Edinburgh, Paris, and Northern Italy.
